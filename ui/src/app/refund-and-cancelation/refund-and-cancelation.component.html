TUTORS OR INSTITUTION REFUND/ CANCEL SUBSCRIPTION POLICY

mayatutors.com will give an opportunity to teachers for using this website with its full features for free for a fixed period. This period is also an opportunity to check whether this site is convenient to them. Therefore, before payment of portal fee / rent, be convinced that whether this site is useful to you.

If a teacher needs a refund/cancel subscription of portal rent for any other reason, that teacher has to inform the same to mail@mayatutors.com in https://mayatutors.com within 7 days after payment. If so, the fee will be refunded within 1 month. But, if the refund is requested after 7 days, refund will not be granted.

while making payment on mayatutors.com, there may be situation that your course does not get enabled and at the same time your bank account was debited. contact your bank or wallet in such situations.

STUDENTS REFUND POLICY
As students need not remit fees directly to mayatutors.com, if students need a refund of the fees given to a selected teacher, request to the respective teacher.
